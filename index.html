Who We Are

Neighbors Building a Stronger Community

Beaumont-Cherry Valley Rotary has been providing community service since 1958. We're one of 63 clubs within Rotary District 5330, united by a simple idea: ordinary people can do extraordinary things when they work together.

Our Guide

The Four-Way Test

Of the things we think, say, or do

1
Is it the truth?
2
Is it fair to all concerned?
3
Will it build goodwill and better friendships?
4
Will it be beneficial to all concerned?

Youth Leadership

Interact Club

Interact brings together young people ages 12–18 to develop leadership skills while discovering the power of service. Our Beaumont Interact Club organizes community and international projects each year.

  • 🌎 Take action in your school and community through hands-on service projects
  • 🤝 Build leadership skills and connect with community mentors
  • 🎓 Earn community service hours and strengthen college applications
  • 🚀 Discover new cultures and promote international understanding

Join Beaumont Interact

Students ages 12–18 are welcome. Contact our Interact advisor to get started.
